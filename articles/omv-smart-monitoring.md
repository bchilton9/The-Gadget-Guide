<h2>Introduction</h2><p>Hard drives and SSDs are the backbone of any NAS, and their failure can result in data loss. OpenMediaVault (OMV) includes built-in support for SMART (Self-Monitoring, Analysis and Reporting Technology) and alerting features to help you spot early signs of failure before they become critical.</p><h2>Step 1: Enable SMART Monitoring</h2><ol><li>Go to <strong>Storage &gt; SMART</strong> in the OMV web interface.</li><li>Click the <strong>Devices</strong> tab.</li><li>Select each of your storage devices, click <strong>Edit</strong>, and enable <strong>Activate SMART monitoring</strong>.</li><li>Click <strong>Apply</strong> to save the settings.</li></ol><h2>Step 2: Set Up SMART Tests</h2><ol><li>Switch to the <strong>Scheduled Tests</strong> tab under <strong>Storage &gt; SMART</strong>.</li><li>Click <strong>Add</strong> and choose the test type (e.g., <code>Short self-test</code>, <code>Long self-test</code>).</li><li>Assign the test to each device and schedule it (e.g., weekly on Sunday at 2 AM).</li><li>Click <strong>Save</strong> and <strong>Apply</strong>.</li></ol><h2>Step 3: Configure Email Notifications</h2><ol><li>Navigate to <strong>System &gt; Notification</strong>.</li><li>Fill in your SMTP email settings, including the sender address, SMTP server, port, and authentication.</li><li>Use a provider like Gmail (enable App Passwords for security) or your ISP's SMTP server.</li><li>Send a test email to verify the configuration.</li></ol><h2>Step 4: Enable SMART Email Alerts</h2><ul><li>Go to <strong>System &gt; Monitoring</strong>.</li><li>Enable notifications for SMART events like <strong>Bad sectors</strong>, <strong>Temperature warnings</strong>, or <strong>Offline uncorrectable sectors</strong>.</li></ul><h2>Tips</h2><ul><li>Review the SMART data regularly, especially the <code>Reallocated Sector Count</code>, <code>Pending Sectors</code>, and <code>Offline Uncorrectable</code> values.</li><li>Set thresholds for temperature to receive alerts when drives overheat.</li><li>Consider weekly long tests for more thorough diagnostics and short tests more frequently.</li></ul><h2>Conclusion</h2><p>SMART monitoring combined with automated email alerts can give you peace of mind that your OpenMediaVault system is proactively watching your drives â€” so you don't have to.</p>
