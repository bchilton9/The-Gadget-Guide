<h2>How to Use Heroic Launcher to Play Epic Games on Steam Deck</h2>

  <p>If you own games on the Epic Games Store or GOG, you can play them on your Steam Deck using <strong>Heroic Launcher</strong>, a free, open-source game launcher that supports Linux, macOS, and Windows.</p>

  <h3>What Is Heroic Launcher?</h3>
  <p>Heroic is a native Linux launcher for both <a href="https://www.epicgames.com/store/en-US/" target="_blank">Epic Games Store</a> and <a href="https://www.gog.com/" target="_blank">GOG</a>. It includes Wine/Proton integration and cloud save support. It’s perfect for the Steam Deck’s Linux-based system.</p>

  <h3>Step 1: Switch to Desktop Mode</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Press the <strong>Steam</strong> button</li>
    <li>Select <strong>Power &gt; Switch to Desktop</strong></li>
  </ol>

  <h3>Step 2: Install Heroic Launcher</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Open the <strong>Discover Software Center</strong></li>
    <li>Search for <strong>Heroic Games Launcher</strong></li>
    <li>Click <strong>Install</strong></li>
  </ol>

  <p>After installation, you can launch Heroic from the desktop menu.</p>

  <h3>Step 3: Log In and Download Games</h3>
  <p>Open Heroic Launcher and log in to your Epic Games or GOG account. You will see a list of your purchased games.</p>

  <ul>
    <li>Select a game and click <strong>Install</strong></li>
    <li>Choose a destination folder (your SD card or internal storage)</li>
    <li>Heroic will automatically apply Proton or Wine for compatibility</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Step 4: Add Heroic Launcher to Steam</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Open <strong>Steam (desktop mode)</strong></li>
    <li>Click <strong>Games &gt; Add a Non-Steam Game</strong></li>
    <li>Select <strong>Heroic Games Launcher</strong> from the list</li>
    <li>Click <strong>Add Selected Programs</strong></li>
  </ol>

  <p>You can rename it to "Epic Games" or "GOG" in Steam for easier navigation.</p>

  <h3>Step 5: Switch Back to Gaming Mode</h3>
  <ol>
    <li>Click <strong>Return to Gaming Mode</strong> from the desktop icon</li>
    <li>Your Heroic shortcut will now appear in the <strong>Non-Steam Games</strong> tab in SteamOS</li>
  </ol>

  <h3>Step 6: Enable Controller Support (Optional)</h3>
  <p>In Gaming Mode:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Select Heroic from your library</li>
    <li>Press the <strong>Options</strong> (☰) button</li>
    <li>Choose <strong>Manage &gt; Controller Layout</strong></li>
    <li>Select a layout or configure your own for better performance</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Troubleshooting Tips</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>If a game won’t launch, try switching between Proton versions in Heroic’s settings</li>
    <li>Use the built-in Wine Manager to update or change versions</li>
    <li>Some games require additional dependencies — check the <a href="https://github.com/Heroic-Games-Launcher/HeroicGamesLauncher/wiki" target="_blank">Heroic Wiki</a> for fixes</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Conclusion</h3>
  <p>With Heroic Launcher, your Steam Deck can access games from multiple storefronts, giving you even more flexibility without needing Windows. It’s a must-have for any Game Pass, GOG, or Epic user looking to maximize the Steam Deck’s potential.</p>
