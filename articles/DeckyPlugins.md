<h2>Decky Loader Plugins</h2>
<ul>
  <li>
    <strong>Plugin:</strong> <a href="index.php#PluginSteamGridDB">SteamGridDB</a> – Custom artwork for Steam and non-Steam games.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Plugin:</strong> <a href="index.php#PluginCSSLoader">CSS Loader</a> – Apply themes to your Steam library.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Plugin:</strong> Animation Changer – Change the startup intro video.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Plugin:</strong> Game Theme Music – Add theme music to your games page.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Plugin:</strong> ProtonDB Badges – Display game compatibility badges from ProtonDB.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Plugin:</strong> Vibrant Deck – Adjust screen saturation on LED models for a more vivid look.
  </li>
</ul>
